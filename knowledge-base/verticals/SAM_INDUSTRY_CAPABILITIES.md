## Tech Startups (Pre-seed / Seed / Early Stage)

**Sales Motion**: B2B Outbound | **Complexity**: Low-Medium | **Cycle**: 7-30 days

### Overview
Early-stage startups have fundamentally different pain points than funded scale-ups. They're founder-led, resource-constrained, and making fast decisions. SAM helps vendors reach these founders effectively.

### Opening Hooks
- "How are you handling sales right now - still founder-led, or starting to build a team?"
- "What's your biggest bottleneck - finding leads, closing, or just time?"
- "Are you doing any outbound, or purely relying on network and inbound?"
- "How are you prioritizing where to spend your limited runway?"

### Pain Points
- Founder wearing every hat (sales, product, support)
- No dedicated sales team or process
- Limited budget for tools and hires
- Need quick wins to show traction for fundraising
- Time scarcity - every hour counts
- Proving product-market fit before scaling
- Building pipeline while building product

### Target Personas
| Title | Priorities | Approach |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Founder/CEO | Traction, runway, fundraising | Fast, practical, ROI |
| Co-founder (Technical) | Building, shipping, efficiency | Technical credibility |
| Head of Growth (if exists) | Pipeline, metrics, experiments | Data-driven, scrappy |
| Founding Sales Rep | Quota, process, tools | Tactical, enablement |

### Language & Terminology
- Runway, burn rate, traction
- PMF (product-market fit)
- Founder-led sales, first 10 customers
- MRR, ARR (even small numbers matter)
- Bootstrapped vs venture-backed
- Pitch deck, investor updates
- YC, Techstars, accelerator

### Benchmarks
| Metric | Below Avg | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|-----------|---------|------|-----------|
| CR Acceptance | <30% | 35-40% | 45%+ | 55%+ |
| Reply Rate | <8% | 12-15% | 20%+ | 30%+ |
| Meeting Book | <5% | 8% | 12%+ | 18%+ |

### Approach Notes
- **Speed matters** - Founders decide fast, don't over-nurture
- **ROI framing** - Every dollar counts, show clear payback
- **Peer proof** - "Other founders at your stage use this"
- **Respect their time** - Be concise, get to the point
- **Understand their stage** - Pre-revenue vs. early revenue changes messaging
- **Network effect** - Founders talk to each other, reputation matters

### Objection Handling
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "No budget right now" | "What if this paid for itself in 30 days? Let's model it." |
| "I'm doing sales myself" | "That's smart at this stage. This frees up 10 hours/week for product and fundraising." |
| "Too early for this" | "Actually, getting sales motion right early prevents expensive mistakes later." |
| "Need to focus on product" | "Totally get it. But customer conversations now shape the product. This accelerates that." |
