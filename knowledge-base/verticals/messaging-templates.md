# Industry Messaging Templates

**Version**: 1.0
**Last Updated**: January 4, 2026

---

## How SAM Uses Templates

SAM adapts these templates based on:

1. Industry-specific language and pain points
2. Buyer persona (CEO, VP, Manager)
3. Channel (LinkedIn CR, LinkedIn DM, Email)
4. Stage in sequence (first touch, follow-up, breakup)

---

## Template Structure

Each industry section includes:

- **Connection Request** (under 300 chars)
- **First Message** (after accept)
- **Follow-up 1** (Day 3)
- **Follow-up 2** (Day 7)
- **Breakup** (Day 14)

Variables: `{first_name}`, `{company}`, `{title}`, `{industry}`, `{mutual_connections}`

## Usage Guidelines

### Personalization Variables

Always replace before sending:

- `{first_name}` ‚Äî Prospect's first name
- `{company}` ‚Äî Company name
- `{title}` ‚Äî Job title
- `{industry}` ‚Äî Industry/vertical
- `{mutual_connections}` ‚Äî Shared connections if any

### Tone Adjustments

- **C-level**: More direct, time-conscious
- **VP/Director**: Results-focused, peer language
- **Manager**: Tactical, day-to-day impact

### Channel Adjustments

- **LinkedIn CR**: Under 300 characters
- **LinkedIn DM**: 3-5 sentences max
- **Email**: Under 100 words
