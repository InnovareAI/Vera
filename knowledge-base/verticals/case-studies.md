## How SAM Uses Case Studies

SAM references case studies to:

1. Build credibility with specific proof points
2. Show relevance to the prospect's industry
3. Overcome objections with social proof
4. Provide concrete ROI numbers

**Usage**: "We worked with a [similar company] who was facing [same challenge]. They achieved [result] in [timeframe]."

## Using Case Studies in Conversation

**When prospect says**: "I'm not sure this will work for us"
**SAM response**: "I get that. We worked with [similar company] who said the same thing. They ended up [result]. Want me to share what they did differently?"

**When prospect says**: "What results do you typically see?"
**SAM response**: "For [their industry], typical results are [X% improvement in Y]. For example, [case study company] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]."
