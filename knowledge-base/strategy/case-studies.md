## SaaS Case Studies

### FinTech Startup - Series A
**Challenge**: Needed to generate pipeline for enterprise sales with limited SDR resources
**Approach**:
- Defined ICP: VP Finance at $50M+ revenue companies
- Personalized messaging around compliance automation
- Multi-touch sequence over 6 weeks

**Results**:
- 34% connection acceptance rate
- 12% reply rate
- 8 qualified meetings in first month
- 2 closed deals ($180K ARR)

**Key Insight**: Tight ICP + industry-specific pain points = higher conversion

---

### HR Tech Scale-up - Series B
**Challenge**: Entering new market segment (mid-market from SMB)
**Approach**:
- A/B tested messaging angles
- Tested value-first vs. pain-agitate approach
- Personalized based on company growth signals

**Results**:
- 28% connection acceptance
- 9% reply rate
- Reduced CAC by 40% vs. previous SDR-led approach

**Key Insight**: A/B testing revealed mid-market prefers ROI framing over problem-agitate

---

### DevTools Company
**Challenge**: Long sales cycles, highly technical buyers
**Approach**:
- Targeted engineering managers and CTOs
- Led with technical credibility (open source contributions)
- Focused on developer productivity metrics

**Results**:
- 41% connection acceptance (high for tech)
- 15% reply rate
- Average deal cycle reduced from 90 to 60 days

**Key Insight**: Technical buyers respond to peer-level credibility, not sales speak

## Professional Services

### Consulting Firm
**Challenge**: Partner-level rainmaking was inconsistent
**Approach**:
- Built partner personal brands on LinkedIn
- Thought leadership + targeted outreach hybrid
- Focused on trigger events (new funding, leadership changes)

**Results**:
- 45% connection acceptance
- 8% meeting book rate
- $2.1M in attributed pipeline in 6 months

**Key Insight**: Combining content with outreach creates compounding effect

---

### Law Firm - Corporate Practice
**Challenge**: Needed to diversify from referral-only business development
**Approach**:
- Identified target: General Counsels at growth companies
- Messaging: Risk-focused, compliance-driven
- Strictly compliant outreach (no solicitation violations)

**Results**:
- 22% connection acceptance
- 4% meeting rate (high for legal)
- 3 new retained clients in first year

**Key Insight**: Compliance-first messaging builds trust with legal buyers

---

### Recruiting Agency
**Challenge**: Candidates and clients both needed - two-sided pipeline
**Approach**:
- Separate campaigns for talent sourcing vs. client acquisition
- Candidate side: Career opportunity framing
- Client side: Speed-to-hire and quality messaging

**Results**:
- Client side: 31% connection, 7% reply
- Candidate side: 48% connection, 22% reply
- Placements up 35% quarter-over-quarter

**Key Insight**: Different messaging for each side of marketplace

## Healthcare & Life Sciences

### Healthcare SaaS
**Challenge**: HIPAA compliance concerns limiting outreach
**Approach**:
- Focused on non-PHI business value
- Emphasized security certifications early
- Targeted operations and IT, not clinical

**Results**:
- 25% connection acceptance
- 6% reply rate
- Compliance-approved messaging template library

**Key Insight**: Lead with security/compliance credentials in regulated industries

---

### Medical Device Company
**Challenge**: Long sales cycle, multiple stakeholders
**Approach**:
- Multi-threading: parallel outreach to different buying committee members
- Personalized by role (clinical vs. administrative vs. financial)
- Account-based approach with coordinated messaging

**Results**:
- 3 enterprise accounts closed ($500K+ each)
- Average 4 contacts per account engaged
- Reduced sales cycle by 25%

**Key Insight**: Multi-threading accelerates complex B2B sales

---

## Manufacturing & Industrial

### Industrial Equipment Manufacturer
**Challenge**: Trade show leads weren't converting
**Approach**:
- Post-event follow-up sequences
- Personalized by booth conversation topic
- Longer nurture sequence (12 touches over 3 months)

**Results**:
- 52% email open rate
- 8% meeting book rate from trade show leads
- $1.2M in attributed revenue

**Key Insight**: Trade show leads need more touches than cold outreach

---

### Supply Chain Software
**Challenge**: COVID disrupted traditional sales motion
**Approach**:
- Pivoted messaging to resilience and visibility
- Targeted VP Operations and Supply Chain leaders
- Offered free assessments as entry point

**Results**:
- 38% connection acceptance
- 11% reply rate
- Pipeline 3x pre-COVID levels

**Key Insight**: Crisis creates urgency - align messaging to current pain
